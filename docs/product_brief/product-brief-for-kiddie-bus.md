# Product Brief: Kiddie Bus

## Overview
Kiddie Bus is a custom application designed for small to medium-sized bus operators in Jamaica, primarily dealing with school transportation. The application aims to replace manual systems like spreadsheets with a more efficient, scalable solution.

## Problem Statement
Current systems rely on manual data entry and management, leading to inefficiencies, errors, and a lack of timely information for parents and operators.

## Target Users
- **Bus Operators**: Manage buses, routes, and schedules.
- **Parents**: Receive timely notifications and updates.
- **Government Authorities**: Require compliance reporting.

## Proposed Solution
Develop a custom app focusing initially on key features:
- **Bus Planning**: Simplified route and schedule management.
- **Parent Notification**: Real-time updates on bus schedules and potential delays.

## Key Features
1. **Bus Planning**: 
   - Route management
   - Schedule optimization
   - Bus assignment tracking

2. **Parent Notification**: 
   - Automatic alerts for delays or emergencies
   - In-app messaging system with parents

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in planning and administrative time
- Increase in parent engagement and satisfaction
- Compliance with government reporting needs
